ESCAPE THE ROOM

An "Escape the Room" game is an immersive and interactive adventure where players are locked in a room and must solve a series of puzzles and challenges to find clues and escape within a set time limit. The game typically involves a themed room with a compelling storyline, and participants must work together to uncover hidden objects, decipher codes, and solve riddles.

JIGSAW PUZZLES

Jigsaw puzzles offer numerous benefits, including improved cognitive skills, enhanced memory, increased concentration, and fine motor skills development. They provide a sense of accomplishment, promote collaboration and social interaction, and can be a source of relaxation. Jigsaw puzzles also contribute to patience, persistence, and may have therapeutic applications in cognitive rehabilitation.

SNAKE AND LADDER

Snake and Ladder, a classic board game, involves players rolling a dice to advance through a numbered grid. The goal is to reach the final square. Ladders propel players forward, while snakes set them back. The game blends luck and strategy, fostering basic counting skills for younger players.
