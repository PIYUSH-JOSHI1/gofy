Favourite Stories That You Love

Storybooks contribute positively to mental health by offering emotional resonance, catharsis, and an escape from real-world stress. They provide diverse perspectives, promote empathy, and reduce stress. Reading stimulates cognitive activity, improves social skills, and inspires motivation. Characters' journeys can foster self-reflection and offer educational insights into coping strategies and emotional well-being.
